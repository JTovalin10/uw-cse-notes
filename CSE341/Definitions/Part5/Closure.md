# CSE341: Closure

A data structure containing a function's code and its **[[CSE341/Definitions/Part5/Dynamic Environment|Lexical Environment]]** at the time of its creation. Closures enable **[[CSE341/Definitions/Part5/First-Class Function|First-Class Functions]]** to maintain access to variables from their defining scope even when called elsewhere.

See: [[CSE341/Trefoil Advanced/First-Class Functions|First-Class Functions in Trefoil]]
