- Set Operations we are able to translate them to proposition logic which we are then able to work with it
$$
A \cup B \quad := \set{x : (x \in A) \lor (x \in B)} \quad \text{Union}
$$
$$
A \cap B \quad := \set{x : (x \in A) \land (x \in B)} \quad \text{Intersection}
$$
$$
A \setminus B \quad := \set{x : (x \in A) \land (x \notin B)} \quad \text{Set Diff}
$$
$$
A \oplus B \quad := \set{x : (x \in A) \oplus (x \in B)} \quad \text{Symmetric Difference}
$$
$$
A^c \quad := \set{x : (x \in U) \land (x \notin A)} \quad \text{Complement} \quad \text{Note: U is the universal set}
$$
