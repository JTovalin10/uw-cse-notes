# CSE 311: Divisibility

#Definition **Divides**: $a \mid b$ means there exists an integer $k$ such that $b = ak$.
$$a \mid b \quad \Rightarrow \quad b = ak, \quad \exists k \in \mathbb{Z}$$

In other words, $a$ divides $b$ if $b$ is an exact integer multiple of $a$ with no remainder.

Example:
$$2 \mid 10 \quad \Rightarrow \quad 10 = 2k \quad \text{(k = 5 is the solution)}$$
