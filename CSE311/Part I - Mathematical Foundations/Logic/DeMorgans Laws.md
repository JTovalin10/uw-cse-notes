# CSE 311: De Morgan's Laws

## Propositional Logic

$$\neg (p \land q) \quad \equiv \quad \neg p \lor \neg q$$
$$\neg (p \lor q) \quad \equiv \quad \neg p \land \neg q$$

## Quantifiers

$$\neg \forall x \quad \equiv \quad \exists x$$
$$\neg \exists x \quad \equiv \quad \forall x$$
