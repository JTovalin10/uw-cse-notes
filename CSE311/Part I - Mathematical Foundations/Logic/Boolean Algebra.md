# CSE 311: Boolean Algebra

Boolean algebra uses a compact **notation** to express logical operators. The following equivalences show how standard logic symbols map to Boolean algebra symbols:

- $\neg p \quad \equiv \quad p'$
- $p \land q \quad \equiv \quad p \cdot q$
- $p \lor q \quad \equiv \quad p + q$
