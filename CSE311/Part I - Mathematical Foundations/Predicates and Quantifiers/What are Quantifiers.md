# CSE 311: Quantifiers

- $\forall x, P(x)$ — "for every $x$, $P$ of $x$"
	- **Universal Quantifier**: asserts that $P(x)$ holds for all $x$ in the domain.
- $\exists x, P(x)$ — "there exists an $x$ such that $P$ of $x$"
	- **Existential Quantifier**: asserts that there is at least one $x$ in the domain for which $P(x)$ holds.
