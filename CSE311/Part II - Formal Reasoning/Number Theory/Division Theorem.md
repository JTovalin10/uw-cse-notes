# CSE 311: Division Theorem

#Definition **Division Theorem**: For integers $a$ and $b$ with $b > 0$, there exist unique integers $q$ and $r$ with $0 \le r < b$ such that:
$$a = qb + r$$

- $q$ is called the **quotient** ($q = a \text{ div } b$).
- $r$ is called the **remainder** ($r = a \bmod b$).
