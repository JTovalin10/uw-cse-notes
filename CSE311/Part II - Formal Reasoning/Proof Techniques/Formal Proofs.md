#Definition Elim or: 
As we know A or B must true then we have to find which one holds
$$
\text{A holds thus:} \quad A \lor B \quad \text{Elim or} \Rightarrow A
$$
$$
\text{neg A holds thus:} \quad A \lor B \quad \text{Elim or} \Rightarrow B
$$
#Definition Elim and:
As we know both hold we can eliminate either or
$$
A \land B \quad \Rightarrow A
$$
$$
A \land B \quad \Rightarrow B
$$
#Definition Intro and:
In order to intro and Q, we need to prove that Q is true
$$
\text{Given G} \quad P \Rightarrow P \land G
$$
$$
\text{Given G} \quad P \Rightarrow G \land P
$$
#Definition Intro or:
If we know P is true then we can add any variable as it will always be true
$$
P \Rightarrow P \lor Q
$$

$$
P \Rightarrow Q \lor P
$$
#Definition Modus Ponens
Given $P \rightarrow Q$ if we know P is true then Q is true

#Definition Direct Proof
If we want to prove $P \Rightarrow Q$ we can use direct proof to Assume P then we have to prove Q
$$
P \Rightarrow Q \quad = \quad P \rightarrow Q
$$
