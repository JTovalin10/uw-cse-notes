# CSE 311: Proof by Calculation

**Proof by calculation** is a technique used to prove an implication where the fact to be shown is an equation or inequality. It works by chaining together known facts and definitions in a sequence of justified steps until the desired result is reached.

- Proves an implication whose conclusion is an equation or inequality.
- Uses known facts and definitions as justification at each step.
  - For example, the fact that `len(nil) = 0` can be used as a definition step.
