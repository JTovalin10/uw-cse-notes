## Length:

$$ \text{len}(\varepsilon) := 0 $$ $$ \text{len}(wa) := \text{len}(w) + 1 \text{ for } w \in \Sigma^*, a \in \Sigma $$

## Concatenation:

$$ x \bullet \varepsilon := x \text{ for } x \in \Sigma^* $$ $$ x \bullet wa := (x \bullet w)a \text{ for } x \in \Sigma^*, a \in \Sigma $$

## Reversal:

$$ \varepsilon^R := \varepsilon $$ $$ (wa)^R := a \bullet w^R \text{ for } w \in \Sigma^*, a \in \Sigma $$

## Number of c's in a string:

$$ #_c(\epsilon) := 0 $$ $$ #_c(wc) := #_c(w) + 1 \text{ for } w \in \sum^* $$ $$ #_c(wa) := #_c(w) \text{ for } w \in \sum^*, a \in \sum, a \neq c $$
