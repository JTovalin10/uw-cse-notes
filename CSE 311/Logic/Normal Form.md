#Definition DNF: Disjunctive Normal Form, finding the true values and adding them together
$$
(a \lor b) \land (\neg a \lor b)
$$
#Definition CNF: Conjuctive Normal Form, finding the false values in a truth value and conjucting them
$$
(a \land b) \lor (\neg a \land b) \lor etc
$$
