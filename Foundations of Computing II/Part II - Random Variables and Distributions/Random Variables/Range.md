# CSE 312: Range ($\Omega_X$)

#Definition The **range** of a [[Random Variables|random variable]] is denoted $\Omega_X$; it is the set of all possible values that $X$ can take on. In this course the range and the [[Support]] of a random variable are treated as the same set.

## Industry Standard Terms

- **Range** $\Omega_X$ → "image of the random variable" / "set of attainable values." Often used interchangeably with **support**.
