# What is it
- state of not being identifiable within a set of subjects
	- you cannot being anonymous by yourself
	- hide your activities among other' similar activities
- Unlinkability of action and identity
- **Unobservability** (hard to achieve)
	- Observer cannot even tell whether a certain action took place or not
