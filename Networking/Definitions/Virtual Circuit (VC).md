## Virtual Circuit (VC)
A logical connection established between source and destination through the network **before** data is sent. Unlike a physical circuit, a VC shares links with other traffic — it's just reserved state in each switch along the path.

See: Virtual Circuit Switching, [[Virtual Circuit Identifier (VCI)]], [[Virtual Path Identifier (VPI)]]
