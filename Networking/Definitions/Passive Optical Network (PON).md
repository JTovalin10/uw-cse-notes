## Passive Optical Network (PON)
A fiber-to-the-home broadband technology that uses a single optical fiber from the [[Internet Service Provider (ISP)|ISP]] and passive optical splitters to serve multiple homes. Key components:
- **[[Optical Line Terminal (OLT)]]** — framing device at the ISP side
- **[[Optical Network Unit (ONU)]]** — endpoint device at each home/business
