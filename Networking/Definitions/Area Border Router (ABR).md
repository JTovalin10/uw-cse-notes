# Area Border Router (ABR)

In [[Open Shortest Path First (OSPF)|OSPF]] [[Routing Areas (OSPF)|routing areas]], an **Area Border Router (ABR)** is a router that connects the [[OSPF Area 0 (Backbone)|backbone]] area (Area 0) to one or more non-backbone areas.

## Key points

- Member of both the backbone and at least one other area
- Summarizes routing information from one area and advertises it to other areas
- Enables inter-area traffic to flow through the backbone
