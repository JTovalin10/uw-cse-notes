f# Common general security goals: “CIA”    
- Confidentiality
	- the concealment of information
		- prevents packet sniffing
		- prevents eavesdropping
		- prevents illegal copying
- Integrity
	- prevention of unauthorized changes
		- unauthorized assumption of another's identity
- Availability
	- the ability to use information or resources
		- system still works despite adversary
			- they overwhelm or crash servers which disrupts infrastructure

# Defining Security Policies
**Asset Name** should have **security property** against **adversary** with **adversary resources**

# towards defenses
**A threat of a response may deter some attackers**
- prevention
	- stop an attack
- detection
	- detect an ongoing or past attack
- Response and Resilience
	- respond to / recover from an attack
