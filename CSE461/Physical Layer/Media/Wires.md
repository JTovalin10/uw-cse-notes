# Twisted Pair
- Most common type of network cable
- Used in LANs (Ethernet) and telephone lines
- Twists reduce electromagnetic interference (EMI) and crosstalk
- Categories determine speed:

| Category | Max Speed | Max Distance | Use |
|----------|-----------|--------------|-----|
| Cat 5 | 100 Mbps | 100m | Legacy |
| Cat 5e | 1 Gbps | 100m | Common |
| Cat 6 | 10 Gbps | 55m | Modern |
| Cat 6a | 10 Gbps | 100m | Data centers |

# Coaxial Cable
- Better shielding than twisted pair
- Higher bandwidth, lower interference
- Used for: cable TV, older Ethernet (10BASE2), cable internet
- Center conductor surrounded by insulation + metal shield

# Comparison
| Property | Twisted Pair | Coaxial |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Cost | Low | Medium |
| Installation | Easy | Moderate |
| Interference | Moderate | Low |
| Bandwidth | Up to 10 Gbps | Up to 10 Gbps |
| Distance | ~100m | ~500m |
