- Messages take space on the wire
![[Pasted image 20260114221556.png]]
- the amount of data in flight is the [[Bandwidth-Delay (BD) Product]]

$$BD = R \times D$$
where $R$ = link rate (bps), $D$ = propagation delay (seconds)
- Units: bits
- Represents how many bits "fill the pipe" at any given moment
