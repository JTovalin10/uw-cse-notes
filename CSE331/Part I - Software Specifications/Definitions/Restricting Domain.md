# CSE331: Restricting Domain

**Restricting Domain**: Techniques to limit the scope of quantifiers to a specific subset of elements.

- Restricting $\forall$
    - Use an **implication**: $\forall x(Cat(x) \land Red(x) \rightarrow Tofu(x))$
- Restricting $\exists$
    - Use $\land$ (**conjunction**): $\exists x(Cat(x) \land Red(x) \land \neg Tofu(x))$
