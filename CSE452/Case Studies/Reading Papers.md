# CSE452: Reading Papers

Guidance for approaching the research papers studied in this course (such as the [[CSE452/Case Studies/Big Table|Big Table]] paper).

- Old papers solve **foundational problems** — they are worth reading because the problems they address still underpin modern systems.
- Go into a paper asking yourself **"why am I reading this paper?"** — establish a goal before you start.
- It is fine to **skip some parts** of a research paper.
	- Sometimes the middle of the paper is not related to your goal.
	- Skipping lets you come back later and engage with the rest once you are interested in it.
