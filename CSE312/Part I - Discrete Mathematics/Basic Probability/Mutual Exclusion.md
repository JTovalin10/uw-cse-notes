# CSE 312: Mutual Exclusion

#Definition **Mutually exclusive** events cannot occur at the same time — $P(A \cap B) = 0$.

Cannot happen at the same time:
- Roll a single die:
	- It lands on 2
	- It lands on 3
		- These cannot happen at the same time
	- It lands on 2
	- Is even
		- Can happen at the same time
