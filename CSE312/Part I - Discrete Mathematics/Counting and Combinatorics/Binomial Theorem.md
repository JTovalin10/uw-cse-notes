# CSE 312: Binomial Theorem

The **Binomial Theorem** gives a formula for expanding a power of a binomial sum. For any non-negative integer $n$:

$$(x+y)^n = \sum_{i=0}^n \binom{n}{i} x^i y^{n-i}$$

Each term in the expansion picks $i$ factors of $x$ and $n-i$ factors of $y$ from the $n$ copies of $(x+y)$, and $\binom{n}{i}$ counts the number of ways to make that choice.
