# CSE 312: Factorial

#Definition **Factorial**: $n! = n \cdot (n-1) \cdot (n-2) \cdots 2 \cdot 1$, with $0! = 1$.

$$n! = \prod_{i=1}^{n} i$$

Factorial counts the number of ways to arrange $n$ distinct objects in a sequence.
