# CSE 312: Sum Rule

#Definition The **Sum Rule** states: the total number of options when choosing from $n$ options in group 1 and $m$ options in group 2, where the groups are **disjoint** (no overlap), is $n + m$.

## Example
If you are deciding where to have dinner in Pelican Town and you can go to Pierre's General Store (where there are 3 meals to choose from) or The Stardrop Saloon (where there are 5 meals to choose from, with no overlap), the total number of choices is:
$$3 + 5 = 8$$
