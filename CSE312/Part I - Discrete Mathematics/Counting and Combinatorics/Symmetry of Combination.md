# CSE 312: Symmetry of Combination

The **symmetry property** of the binomial coefficient states:

$$\binom{n}{k} = \frac{n!}{(n-k)!\, k!} = \binom{n}{n-k}$$

Choosing $k$ items to include is equivalent to choosing which $n-k$ items to leave out, so the two counts are always equal.
