# CSE 312: Sequences

- **Sequences** means order matters — if we are bound to $\{n, m, k\}$, all ways to represent the sequence are distinct, so $\{n, m, k\}$ is not the same as $\{m, k, n\}$.
- **Distinct** means you cannot repeat elements.
- The size of a sequence of $n$ distinct elements is defined by [[Factorial]]: $n!$
