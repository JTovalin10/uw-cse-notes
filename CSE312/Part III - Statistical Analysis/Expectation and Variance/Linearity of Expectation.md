# CSE 312: Linearity of Expectation

For any two random variables $X$ and $Y$ (doesn't have to be independent):
$$E[X + Y] = E[X] + E[Y]$$

With constants (you can also pull out constants):
$$E[aX + bY + c] = aE[X] + bE[Y] + c$$
