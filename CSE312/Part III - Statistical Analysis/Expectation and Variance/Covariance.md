# CSE 312: Covariance

#Definition **Covariance** measures how two random variables vary together — if $X$ turns out "big," how likely is it that $Y$ will also be "big"?
$$\text{Cov}(X, Y) = \mathbb{E}[(X - \mathbb{E}[X])(Y - \mathbb{E}[Y])] = \mathbb{E}[XY] - \mathbb{E}[X]\mathbb{E}[Y]$$
