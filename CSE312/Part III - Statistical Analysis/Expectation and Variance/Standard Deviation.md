# CSE 312: Standard Deviation

#Definition The **standard deviation** is found by $\sqrt{\sigma^2}$, which is the square root of the variance. If we want to find the total standard deviation of $n$ items we simply do $\sqrt{n \cdot \sigma^2}$.
