# CSE 312: Support

#Definition The **support** of a [[Random Variables|random variable]] is the set of values that $X$ can take with non-zero probability. The support is equivalent to the [[Range]] of the random variable, and is denoted $\Omega_X$.

$$\text{Support} = \text{Range} = \Omega_X$$

## Industry Standard Terms

- **Support** → standard statistics term; the support of a distribution is the smallest closed set whose complement has probability zero.
